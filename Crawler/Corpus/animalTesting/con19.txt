 Animals can suffer like humans do, so it is speciesism to experiment on them while we refrain from experimenting on humans.
  All suffering is undesirable, whether it be in humans or animals. Discriminating against animals because they do not have the cognitive ability, language, or moral judgment that humans do is no more justifiable than discriminating against human beings with severe mental impairments.
