 Major medical organizations state that vaccines are safe.
  These organizations include: CDC, Food and Drug Administration (FDA), Institute of Medicine (IOM), American Medical Association (AMA), American Academy of Pediatrics (AAP), UNICEF, US Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), World Health Organization (WHO), Public Health Agency of Canada, Canadian Paediatric Society, National Foundation for Infectious Diseases (NFID), and American Academy of Family Physicians (AAFP).  The WHO states, "Vaccines are very safe."  The US Department of Health and Human Services states, "Vaccines are some of the safest medical products available."
